what makes nappr special?


user empathy

we're intimately familiar with the needs and problems of our users, because being busy students, we're everyday commuters too.

ease of use

no bells and whistles here. nappr's fuss free UI is designed to be so simple and intuitive even your grandmother could use it.

reliability

you can count on nappr to provide you with an accurate location-based audio vibration alert every time. we've got your back, so you can snooze in peace.

continuous development

we're dedicated to making the app as robust as possible, and have big plans for the future which may or may not include world domination.
